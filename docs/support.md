# Flash Plan — Support

Flash Plan turns a quick LiDAR scan of a room into a clean, top-down 2D floorplan you can
save to your Photos.

## Requirements

Flash Plan needs an iPhone with a LiDAR sensor (iPhone 12 Pro or newer Pro model) and
iOS 17 or later. On devices without LiDAR, scanning is unavailable.

## How to use it

1. Open the app and tap to start a scan.
2. Slowly move around the room so the camera can see every wall, door, and window.
3. When the room is captured, the app shows your 2D floorplan.
4. Tap save to add the floorplan image to your photo library.

Accuracy is a rough guide, not a survey-grade measurement.
