# Flash Plan — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 24 July 2026_

Flash Plan is designed to work entirely on your device. It does not collect, transmit,
sell, or share any personal data.

## What the app accesses

**Camera and LiDAR.** Flash Plan uses the camera and LiDAR sensor only while you are
actively scanning a room, so it can measure the space and build a floorplan. The scan is
processed on your device by Apple's RoomPlan framework. Camera frames are never recorded,
stored, or sent anywhere.

**Photos.** When you choose to save a floorplan, the app adds a single image to your photo
library. It only adds images you explicitly export; it never reads, browses, or uploads
your existing photos.

## What the app does not do

- No accounts, no sign-in.
- No network connections, no servers, no cloud.
- No analytics, no advertising, no tracking.
- No third-party SDKs.

## Data retention

The app stores nothing about you. The only output is the floorplan image you choose to
save to your own photo library, which remains entirely under your control.

## Children's privacy

Flash Plan collects no data from anyone, including children.
